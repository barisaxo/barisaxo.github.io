Inversions

To invert means to go inside-out, or upside-down.

   Simply said, you move another note to the bottom of the stack.


You can invert an interval by swapping the quality and quantity:

Quantity:
•  Unison <-> Octave
•  2nd <-> 7th
•  3rd <-> 6th
•  4th <-> 5th
Quality:
•  Major <-> Minor
•  Augmented <-> Diminished
•  Perfect <-> Perfect
