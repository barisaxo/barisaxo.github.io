Intervals

•An interval is the distance between two notes.


Intervals have two elements:

Quantity: The letter to letter distance.

•Unisons (1sts)
•2nds
•3rds
•4ths
•5ths
•6ths
•7ths
•Octaves (8ths)


Quality: The overall distance in half steps,
in combination with the Quantity.

•Major
•Minor
•Augmented
•Diminished
•Perfect


•2nds, 3rds, 6ths, and 7ths cannot be perfect.

•Unisons and Octaves should only be perfect.

•4ths and 5ths cannot be major or minor.
